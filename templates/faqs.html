Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?

It's simple! To have the best experience on Worldsmits, sign up and create a profile. From there, you can explore our user's groups and events and begin meeting fellow worldbuilders.

Can I change my password?

Yes. Go to your profile, click on 'Settings' and click on 'Change passowrd'. Follow the instructions and click 'Save'.

How can I see my groups and events?

Once logged in, go to your profile and your groups and events will be displayed on your profile. Alternatively, on the Search page you can filter the events (by organiser/attendee) and groups (by admin/member of) to show the groups you have involvement in.

How do I create a group or event?

Go to your profile, navigate to the section you want (group or event) and click on the 'Create group/event' button.

How do I leave a group or event?

Go to the event/group and click the 'Leave' button. It will then be removed from your profile.

How do I delete a group or event that I've created?

Go to your event or group that you've created, go to the 'Settings' and click the 'Delete' button. It will then be removed from your profile, and every user's profile who are involved with it.

How do I follow another user?

Go to the profile you wish to follow and click the 'Follow' button.

Can I delete my profile?

Yes, go to your profile 'Settings' and click 'Delete my profile'.
