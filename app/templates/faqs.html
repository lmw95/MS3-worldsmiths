Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?

It's simple! To have the best experience on Worldsmits, sign up and create a profile. From there, you can explore our user's groups and begin meeting fellow worldbuilders.

Can I change my password?

Yes. Go to your profile, click on 'Settings' and click on 'Change passowrd'. Follow the instructions and click 'Save'.

How can I see my groups and following?

Once logged in, go to your profile and your groups and following will be displayed on your profile.

How do I create a group?

Go to your profile, navigate to the profile info and click 'Create group'

How do I leave a group?

Go to the event/group and click the 'Leave' button. It will then be removed from your profile.

How do I delete a group that I've created?

Go to your event or group that you've created, and click the 'Delete' button. It will then be removed from your profile, and every user's profile who are involved with it.

How do I follow another user?

Go to the profile you wish to follow and click the 'Follow' button.

Can I delete my profile?

Yes, go to your profile 'Settings' and click 'Delete my profile'.
